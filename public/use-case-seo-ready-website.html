The Challenge

U.S. Auction Brokers wanted to maximize conversion rates, improve customer journey efficiency, and gain deeper insights into campaign performance across all touchpoints.

Our Approach

  • Landing Page Optimization: A/B tested 8 layouts, improved CTAs, streamlined checkout flow
  • Website Speed: Mobile optimization, load time reduced from 2.1s → 0.8s
  • Google Ads: Campaigns restructured by product category, dynamic product ads implemented
  • Email Marketing: Automated sequences for cart abandonment and post-purchase engagement
  • Analytics Dashboard: Custom performance dashboard tracking all touchpoints and revenue attribution
  • Retargeting: Pixel-based retargeting across search and social

The Results

  • Conversion rate: 1.2% → 3.8% (+216%)
  • Revenue per visitor: +240%
  • Email marketing: 22% of total online sales
  • ROAS: 2.1x → 5.8x
  • Average order value: +28%
