Design direction

Closer to the original tone

The live Dokon website uses a refined, art-led tone. This mini website keeps that editorial warmth through serif typography, quieter colors and more thoughtful content blocks, but trims the structure down to what a single link page really needs.

Source-inspiredBased on the live Dokon site’s overall mood, content themes and multidisciplinary structure.
