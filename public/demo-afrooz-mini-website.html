Design direction

Mini website, not a full website

This concept takes cues from link-in-bio experiences such as Linktree and HeyLink, but keeps a more premium brand feel. The goal is to give Afrooz one elegant public link for Instagram bio, easy customer contact and quick store discovery.

Brand-led paletteWhite, turquoise and soft mint surfaces with a cleaner premium feel as requested.
Grounded contentStore level, hours and contact path are aligned to the Oman Avenues Mall listing for Afrouz.
Flexible updatesCollection blocks can be swapped later with real Instagram product photos when supplied as downloadable assets.
