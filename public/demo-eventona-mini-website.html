Design direction

More energy, less friction

This version is intentionally not website-like. It behaves more like a premium booking and information page that can sit behind one Instagram bio link. The structure is short, visual and direct, inspired by link-in-bio systems and event-brand landing patterns.

Orange-led brand feelThe page takes its core personality from the Eventona visual identity you shared.
Simple booking pathKey actions are near the top so interested visitors can move fast.
Good for paid or organic trafficOne page can serve Instagram, WhatsApp shares and direct link sharing.

Why this works as a mini website

Short and memorableNo heavy navigation or unnecessary sections.
Clear informationCustomers immediately understand the business and what to do next.
Made for a single bio linkA cleaner path from Instagram discovery to inquiry, without the cost of a full custom website.
