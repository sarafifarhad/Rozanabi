What is EVENTONA?

EVENTONA is a local discovery platform for Oman that brings weekend plans, nearby events, hidden spots, and curated experiences into one simple place. From family outings and sports activities to cultural programs, music nights, business meetups, Wadi trips, landmarks, and community events, EVENTONA helps people decide where to go and what to do next.
