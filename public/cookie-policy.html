Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Rozana Business Intelligence LLC uses cookies and similar technologies on this website to provide essential site functionality, remember visitor choices, understand how the website is used, improve performance, and support future analytics or marketing tools where consent is required.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your browser or device when you visit a website. They help the website function, remember preferences, measure usage, and support certain security, analytics, or marketing features. Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, tags, pixels, or scripts.

2. Why Rozana uses cookies

We use cookies for practical website operation and visitor experience. The website may use cookies to:

  • Keep essential website functions working properly.
  • Remember your cookie preference choices.
  • Improve website speed, stability, layout, and usability.
  • Understand which pages are visited and how content performs.
  • Prepare for future analytics, campaign measurement, and marketing integrations when enabled.
  • Protect the contact form from spam or automated misuse when security verification is enabled.

3. Cookie categories we use

Essential cookies

Essential cookies are required for core website functionality, security, accessibility, consent storage, and basic user experience. These cookies cannot be disabled through the website preference panel because the site may not work correctly without them.

Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use the website. They may support Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager measurement for page views, popular content, user journeys, device type, traffic source, campaign traffic, form events and website performance. Analytics cookies are used only when enabled by the visitor where consent is required.

Marketing cookies

Marketing cookies may support Google Ads conversion measurement, remarketing readiness, ad personalization signals, audience building, paid-media performance review, or future campaign tracking. These cookies are not required for basic website use and are only enabled when the visitor accepts them where consent is required.

Functional cookies

Functional cookies may remember non-essential preferences or improve convenience, such as saved choices or interface preferences. If functional cookies are added later, they will be handled according to the website preference model.

4. Current cookie preference options

The cookie banner gives visitors three practical choices:

  • Accept All: Allows essential, analytics, and marketing categories where configured.
  • Reject All: Keeps only essential cookies active.
  • Manage Choices: Allows visitors to choose categories before saving preferences.

5. How cookie choices are stored

Your cookie preference choice may be stored in your browser using local storage or an essential consent cookie. This helps the website remember your selection so the banner does not appear repeatedly on every page. If you clear your browser data, change browsers, use a different device, or browse in private mode, your preferences may need to be set again.

6. Third-party tools

Rozana may use or prepare for third-party tools such as analytics platforms, advertising platforms, embedded media, social media integrations, or form security services. These providers may place cookies or similar technologies when their tools are active and permitted by your consent choice.

Examples of possible third-party categories include Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics 4, Google Ads conversion or remarketing tools, analytics tools, marketing pixels, social media embeds, video embeds, and security verification tools such as Cloudflare Turnstile.

7. Contact form and security cookies

The contact form may use security technology to reduce spam and automated submissions. If Cloudflare Turnstile or a similar verification tool is activated, it may process technical information needed to confirm that the submission is legitimate. This type of processing is generally treated as essential for form security.

8. Managing cookies in your browser

You can also manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies, delete existing cookies, or receive notifications before cookies are stored. Blocking all cookies may affect website functionality.

9. Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when website functionality changes, new analytics tools are added, marketing integrations are introduced, or legal and operational requirements change. Updated versions will be published on this page.

10. Contact us

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy or how Rozana handles website privacy preferences, you can contact us at info@rozanabi.com.
